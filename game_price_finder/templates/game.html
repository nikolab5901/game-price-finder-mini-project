Buyer checklist
  • Match the exact edition (base, GOTY, deluxe, remaster) before comparing prices.
  • Separate physical, digital license, and reseller keys — protections and restrictions differ.
  • Use sold / completed listings (not only the cheapest active ask) when benchmarking.
  • Confirm region, platform family, and whether DLC or codes were already redeemed.
Seller checklist
  • Anchor your ask to recent sold comps with the same condition and completeness (CIB, loose, digital).
  • Disclose scratches, repro boxes, missing manuals, or redeemed DLC plainly to reduce disputes.
  • Remember fees, shipping, and taxes change net proceeds versus headline comps.
  • Use clear photos of labels and media — buyers often verify authenticity against reference shots.
